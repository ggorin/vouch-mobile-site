The Price Difference is Huge

This is the most important comparison point: Vouch Mobile saves you $25/month compared to Cricket's unlimited plan, while using the exact same AT&T network.

Annual Savings: $300

Switching from Cricket to Vouch saves you $300 per year - that's real money back in your pocket for identical network coverage.

Same Network, Different Price

Since both carriers use AT&T's network, you get:

  • Identical coverage maps
  • Same 5G access
  • Same call quality
  • Same network reliability

The only difference is Cricket has retail stores while Vouch is online-only. If you're comfortable ordering online (like most people today), there's no reason to pay Cricket's premium.

Why Cricket Costs More

Cricket's higher prices fund their retail store network and marketing. As an online-only carrier, Vouch passes those savings directly to you. You get AT&T's network without paying for Cricket's overhead.

Who Should Choose Vouch Mobile?

  • You want to save money on the same AT&T network
  • You're comfortable ordering online
  • You don't need in-person store support
  • You want straightforward, low pricing

Who Should Choose Cricket?

  • You prefer in-store support and assistance
  • You want to buy phones in a retail store
  • You're not comfortable with online-only service

The Verdict

Vouch Mobile is the clear winner for anyone comfortable with online service. You get identical AT&T network coverage for $25 less per month. That's $300/year in savings for the same service.
