Plans & Pricing

Our Premium Unlimited plan is $30/month plus applicable taxes and regulatory fees. This includes unlimited talk, text, data, and mobile hotspot.

No hidden fees! Your monthly cost is the plan price ($30) plus any applicable taxes and regulatory fees, which vary by location. There are no activation fees, no line access fees, and no surprise charges.

Unlimited means no overage charges and no hard data caps. During times of network congestion, data speeds may be temporarily reduced. Video streaming may be optimized to standard definition to ensure a good experience for all users.

Yes! You can add additional lines to your account. Each line is $30/month with the same unlimited features. Manage all lines from a single account.

Coverage & Network

Vouch Mobile operates on AT&T's nationwide network, providing access to 5G and LTE coverage across the United States. This means you get the same great coverage as AT&T customers.

Yes! 5G access is included at no extra cost. You'll automatically connect to 5G where available with a compatible device.

Yes, WiFi calling is included free with your plan. This allows you to make and receive calls over a WiFi connection when cellular coverage is limited.

Activation & Compatibility

No! Vouch Mobile is a prepaid service, so we never run credit checks. Everyone is approved regardless of credit history. Sign up in minutes.

Yes! We support eSIM activation for compatible devices. This means you can activate your service instantly without waiting for a physical SIM card.

Billing & Account

No contracts, ever! Vouch Mobile is month-to-month. You can cancel anytime without any cancellation fees or penalties.

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover). You can set up autopay for convenient monthly billing.

We offer refunds within the first 7 days of service if you're not satisfied. Contact our support team to request a refund. After 7 days, unused service is non-refundable but you can cancel anytime.
