About Vouch Mobile

Making quality wireless affordable for everyone

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to reliable, affordable wireless service. That's why we created Vouch Mobile—a simple, straightforward phone plan that doesn't discriminate based on your credit score or lock you into long-term contracts.

What We Stand For

Affordability

Premium wireless shouldn't cost a fortune. We keep our prices low so you can keep more money in your pocket.

Inclusivity

No credit checks means everyone is welcome. Your financial history shouldn't determine your access to communication.

Simplicity

One plan, one price, no surprises. We believe in transparent, straightforward service without the fine print.

Why We Started Vouch Mobile

The wireless industry has long been dominated by carriers who prioritize profits over people. Complex pricing, hidden fees, credit checks, and long-term contracts have made it difficult for many Americans to get the service they need.

We saw an opportunity to do things differently. By leveraging the power of AT&T's network through our partnership, we're able to offer the same great coverage at a fraction of the price—without the barriers that keep people from getting connected.

Vouch Mobile is for the budget-conscious family looking to save money, the young professional building their credit, and everyone in between who deserves honest, affordable wireless service.

Powered by Reach Mobile

Vouch Mobile service is provided by Reach Mobile, a trusted MVNO (Mobile Virtual Network Operator) operating on AT&T's nationwide network.

This partnership allows us to offer reliable coverage at competitive prices while maintaining the quality and service you expect.
