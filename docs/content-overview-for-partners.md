## Key Messaging & Value Propositions

### Primary Messages
1. **Affordable:** $30/month unlimited vs. $100+ at major carriers
2. **No Barriers:** No credit check, no contracts
3. **Premium Network:** Full AT&T 5G/LTE coverage nationwide
4. **Simple:** Two plan options, transparent pricing

### Target Audiences
- Budget-conscious consumers
- People with poor/no credit history
- Students and young adults
- Seniors on fixed incomes
- Gig economy workers
- Small business owners
- Immigrants and new Americans
- Frequent travelers

### Competitive Advantages
- AT&T network quality at MVNO pricing
- No credit check required
- Month-to-month flexibility
- 5G included at no extra cost
- Both physical SIM and eSIM support
- Free number porting
