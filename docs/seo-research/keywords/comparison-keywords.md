# Comparison Keywords Research - Phone Plans & MVNO Alternatives

## Executive Summary

This document contains comprehensive keyword research for phone plan comparison and MVNO alternative searches. The data reflects 2025 market trends and buyer intent signals for individuals evaluating mobile carriers and considering switching from major carriers to MVNOs.

**Key Insight:** Comparison keywords show high commercial intent. Users searching "X vs Y" carrier terms are typically 60-80% closer to conversion than generic "best phone plan" searches. These keywords represent the highest-priority content opportunities for Vouch's positioning against established MVNOs and major carriers.

---

## Primary Comparison Keywords

### Direct Competitor vs Competitor

These keywords represent users actively evaluating two specific carriers. High search volume, strong buyer intent.

#### Tier 1: Mint Mobile Comparisons
- **"mint mobile vs visible"** - Highest volume comparison
  - Intent: Budget-conscious users comparing T-Mobile MVNO vs Verizon MVNO
  - Competitor density: Very high (10+ major comparison articles)
  - Best positioning: Emphasize Vouch's unlimited pricing vs Mint's data tiers

- **"mint mobile alternative"**
  - Estimated volume: 445.3K (mint mobile keyword alone shows this traffic level)
  - Related keywords: "mint mobile alternatives 2025", "best mint mobile alternative"
  - Intent: Users considering switching away from Mint Mobile
  - Entry point: Vouch can target dissatisfied Mint customers

- **"mint mobile vs cricket"**
  - Intent: Comparing T-Mobile MVNO to AT&T prepaid
  - Pricing angle: Mint requires upfront payment vs Cricket's monthly flexibility

#### Tier 2: Visible Comparisons
- **"visible vs mint mobile"** (reverse of above)
- **"visible alternatives"**, **"visible wireless alternative"**
  - Intent: Verizon MVNO users seeking options
  - Opportunity: Vouch can emphasize unlimited plans vs Visible's speed-capped options

#### Tier 3: Prepaid Carrier Comparisons
- **"cricket wireless vs metro by t-mobile"**
  - High volume comparison
  - Intent: Users comparing AT&T vs T-Mobile prepaid options
  - Vouch positioning: Superior unlimited plans and flexibility

- **"boost mobile vs metro by t-mobile"**
  - Intent: T-Mobile MVNO options comparison
  - Key differentiator: Vouch's unlimited data vs Boost's limitations

- **"tello vs cricket wireless"**
  - Intent: Budget plan flexibility (Tello) vs family discounts (Cricket)
  - Opportunity: Vouch can compete on both fronts

### Major Carrier to MVNO/Alternative

Users evaluating leaving expensive major carriers for cheaper options.

#### "Cheaper Than" Keywords
