# High-Intent Commercial Keywords for Vouch Phone Plans

This document contains keyword research for phone plan content targeting high commercial intent searchers. Keywords are organized by category with estimated commercial value indicators, competitor presence, and conversion signals.

## Research Limitations & Notes

### Data Collected

- Search result prevalence from major carriers, MVNOs, and review sites
- Pricing data from 2025-2026 market offerings
- Competitive MVNO listing from comparison sites
- Feature and pricing positioning from provider websites

### Data NOT Collected (Due to Tool Limitations)

- **Exact Monthly Search Volume** - Requires Google Keyword Planner or similar (subscription tools)
- **Cost-Per-Click (CPC) Rates** - Specific to Google Ads auctions; varies by geography and time
- **Keyword Difficulty Scores** - Requires SEO tools like SEMrush, Ahrefs, Moz
- **Click-Through Rates (CTR)** - Requires GSC data or third-party tools
- **Conversion Rate Data** - Not publicly available

### Recommendations for Further Research

1. **Use Google Keyword Planner** - Free tool for registered Google Ads accounts
   - Provides search volume ranges and CPC estimates
   - Most accurate for local/US market

2. **SEO Tool Analysis** - Use trial access to:
   - SEMrush Keyword Magic Tool (keyword difficulty, search volume)
   - Ahrefs Keyword Explorer (SERP analysis, competition metrics)
   - Moz Keyword Explorer (difficulty scores)

3. **Competitive Monitoring** - Track:
   - Which keywords drive traffic to competitor sites
   - Content strategy of top-ranking pages
   - Seasonal variations in search interest
