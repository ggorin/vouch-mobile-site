## How to Cite

When writing content, use these patterns:

**For statistics:**
> According to [OpenSignal's 2025 Mobile Network Report](link), AT&T covers 99% of the US population.

**For comparisons:**
> [WhistleOut](link) ranks the top unlimited plans under $30...

**For technical info:**
> The FCC requires carriers to [unlock phones after 60 days](link)...

**DO NOT:**
- Link directly to mintmobile.com, visible.com, cricketwireless.com, etc.
- Use competitor marketing materials as sources
- Deep link to competitor pricing pages

**DO:**
- Use neutral comparison sites (WhistleOut, Tom's Guide, Clark)
- Cite government sources (FCC, FTC)
- Reference independent research (OpenSignal, Ookla)
- Use consumer review aggregators (BBB stats, ConsumerAffairs)
