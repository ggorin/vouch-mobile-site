## Strategy Overview

### Target Audiences
1. **Budget-conscious consumers** - Price-sensitive shoppers looking for deals
2. **Credit-challenged individuals** - People who can't get postpaid plans
3. **BYOD users** - People with unlocked phones looking for service
4. **Carrier switchers** - Frustrated customers from major carriers
5. **First-time phone plan buyers** - Young adults, immigrants, newly independent

### Primary Keyword Themes
- No credit check phone plans
- Cheap/affordable unlimited plans
- Prepaid phone plans
- MVNO / alternative carriers
- AT&T coverage alternatives
- [City/State] phone plans

### State Page FAQ Guidelines

**CRITICAL:** FAQ schema should only be used for genuine questions users ask, not promotional content. Google penalizes FAQ schema misuse.

**Required Questions (adapt to each state):**

1. **Coverage question**
   "Does Vouch Mobile work in [State]?"
   - Confirm AT&T network coverage
   - Mention major cities
   - Link to coverage checker

2. **Network question**
   "What network does Vouch use in [State]?"
   - Explain AT&T MVNO relationship
   - Coverage percentage
   - 5G availability

3. **Pricing question**
   "How much does Vouch cost in [State]?"
   - $30/mo unlimited
   - No state-specific taxes added (or clarify if there are)
   - Compare to state average

4. **Credit check question**
   "Do I need a credit check for Vouch in [State]?"
   - No credit check required
   - Prepaid model explanation

5. **Switching question**
   "How do I switch to Vouch in [State]?"
   - Port number process
   - Timeline (same day activation)
   - Link to switching guide

**Optional State-Specific Questions:**

6. "Is Vouch good for [major city] residents?"
7. "Does Vouch work in rural [State]?"
8. "How does Vouch compare to [local competitor] in [State]?"


## SEO Technical Requirements

### On-Page
- Unique title tags (< 60 chars)
- Meta descriptions (< 160 chars)
- H1 with primary keyword
- Schema markup (FAQ, HowTo, LocalBusiness)
- Internal linking between related pages
- Mobile-responsive design
- Fast loading (< 3s)

### Content Quality
- 1,000+ words for guides
- 800+ words for state pages
- 600+ words for comparison pages
- Unique content per page (no duplicate templates)
- Local/specific information per geo page
- Updated annually minimum

---

## Measurement & KPIs

### Traffic Goals
- 6-month goal: 10,000 organic sessions/month
- 12-month goal: 50,000 organic sessions/month
- Top 3 ranking for 25+ primary keywords

### Conversion Goals
- 2% conversion rate on commercial pages
- 0.5% conversion rate on informational pages
- 50+ sign-ups/month from organic traffic

---

## Notes

- Per Google's John Mueller: Up to 50 state location pages is acceptable
- Avoid thin content - each page needs unique, valuable information
- Update geo pages with local stats, testimonials, and events
- Build backlinks through PR, guest posts, and partnerships
- Monitor rankings weekly, adjust strategy monthly
