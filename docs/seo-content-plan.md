## Strategy Overview

### Target Audiences
1. **Budget-conscious consumers** - Price-sensitive shoppers looking for deals
2. **Credit-challenged individuals** - People who can't get postpaid plans
3. **BYOD users** - People with unlocked phones looking for service
4. **Carrier switchers** - Frustrated customers from major carriers
5. **First-time phone plan buyers** - Young adults, immigrants, newly independent

### Primary Keyword Themes
- No credit check phone plans
- Cheap/affordable unlimited plans
- Prepaid phone plans
- MVNO / alternative carriers
- AT&T coverage alternatives
- [City/State] phone plans

## SEO Technical Requirements

### On-Page
- Unique title tags (< 60 chars)
- Meta descriptions (< 160 chars)
- H1 with primary keyword
- Schema markup (FAQ, HowTo, LocalBusiness)
- Internal linking between related pages
- Mobile-responsive design
- Fast loading (< 3s)

### Content Quality
- 1,000+ words for guides
- 800+ words for state pages
- 600+ words for comparison pages
- Unique content per page (no duplicate templates)
- Local/specific information per geo page
- Updated annually minimum

### Traffic Goals
- 6-month goal: 10,000 organic sessions/month
